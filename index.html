ABOUT OSASU

A new-generation builder

At 29, Osasu Idahosa represents a rare blend of youth, polish and intention. He is part of a new generation of Nigerian founders choosing to build with both taste and substance, shaping a business presence rooted in excellence, faith and long-range vision.
